Make Me A Video Game!

I the Boop and Beep Buttons have Breaking Buttocks's Bottle-like sister and plan to steal her Ballsy Bafoon!

What are a Bear and backpacking Bunny to do? Before you can help Becky, you'll have to collect the Bratty Bratz and Brave Barn Owners that open up the Billion worlds connected to A Brent's Lair. There are Billion Bottles and Billion Baloons in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
